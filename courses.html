1 YEAR OF FOCUS LEARNING

Divided into 3 terms: Fundamentals, expanding visual library, and develop your story driven portfolio.

Term 1: Developing Fundamentals

Use perspective, lighting, anatomy, materials and forms to visually communicate designs. Understanding how to draw objects in three-dimensional space. Learn camera perspective for an interesting shot.

Term 2: Expand Visual Library

Extract design languages by exploring history, culture and the nature world. Generate new ideas based on the realities around us, by combining style that you can see everywhere in our world.

Term 3: Develop Your Portfolio

Leverage knowledge from the previous terms to develop fresh and marketable designs. Graduate with a solid and industry-ready portfolio. Start your new beginning as a professional artist.
