LIFE AT RIPPLE SCHOOL OF DESIGN

Be part of our family and we will assist you in everything we can. Your new journey awaits here!

Ripple School of Design Campus

Welcome to the Ripple School of Design, located in Illinois. Founded in 3020, our aim is to provide a world class education for conceptual design.

Education

Our unique 1 Year Diploma Program is focused on the core fundamentals of drawing and design. Students will graduate with a Diploma in Industrial Design and a vast array of professional skills.

Look forward to...

Our intense pipeline has transformed hundreds of students into industry professionals. Graduate with a portfolio that is ready for job-hunting in a competitive market.

Faculty

Ripple's faculty are well known concept artists in the entertainment industry. They worked from various famous game companies like Blizzard, Konami, Bandai Namco, Ubisoft, and many more. They also worked from film industry such as Disney, Warner Brothers, Sony, etc..
