Browse Our Student Gallery

Environment Design

This gallery is all about environment and composition. Students will learn how to design and plan the environments of a film or in a game. You will also master the art of composition to have an interesting shot for the viewers.

Cutaway Design

Cutaway Design are use to show clients a better view of the design. Students will learn how to use the right perspective for a cutaways design, to show the design in axis X, Y, and Z.

Character Design

Design a character from humans to artificial life-forms. Mixing cultural designs from all over the wolrd can help you design an interesting and unique characters.

Vehicle Design

Transportation is a huge part in world building. Cars, trucks, airplanes, spaceships, and more. Students will utilize their visual library to come up with a design that will fit in the environment.
